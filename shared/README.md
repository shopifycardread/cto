## Brand DNA
Clad is modern, premium, and fashion-forward. We use "Warm Minimalism" to make AI styling feel human, sophisticated, and effortless.

**Colors:**
- Primary: Clad Black (#1A1A1A)
- Secondary: Silk Beige (#F5F0E1)
- Accents: Cognac (#A67C52), Sage (#B2BDAC)

# Clad Brand Identity & Style Guide

## 1. Brand Vision
Clad is an AI-powered personal stylist and wardrobe OS. It is modern, premium, and fashion-forward. We provide "Warm Minimalism" — a sophisticated but accessible experience.

## 2. Tone of Voice
- **Confident:** We know fashion.
- **Intelligent:** Data-driven recommendations that feel personal.
- **Effortless:** Styling should be easy, not a chore.
- **Premium:** Quality over quantity.

## 3. Color Palette
- **Clad Black (#1A1A1A):** Primary text and grounding elements.
- **Silk Beige (#F5F0E1):** Primary background for a warm, high-end feel.
- **Cognac (#A67C52):** Accent for CTAs and highlights.
- **Sage (#B2BDAC):** Secondary accent for soft UI elements.
- **Off-White (#FAF9F6):** Secondary background and card color.

## 4. Typography
- **Headings:** *Editorial Serif* (e.g., Playfair Display or a custom serif) – Communicates fashion and authority.
- **Body/UI:** *Geometric Sans-Serif* (e.g., Inter or Montserrat) – Communicates modern technology and readability.

## 5. Logo
- **Symbol:** Abstract folded garment or 'C' monogram.
- **Wordmark:** Sophisticated serif 'Clad'.
- **Usage:** Logo-concept-1 is the primary identity.

## 6. Component Principles
- **Soft Shadows:** Depth without harshness.
- **Generous White Space:** Focus on the clothing items.
- **Rounded Corners (8-12px):** Modern and approachable.
- **Imagery:** High-quality, well-lit photography of clothes.
